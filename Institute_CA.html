Institute for the Creative Arts

Situated at the Mosta Campus, the Institute for the Creative Arts offers a growing selection of courses, ranging from fine art on paper and canvas, to digital design and printing, even creative media production, photography, journalism, fashion, performing arts, and cultural heritage skills.
The institute works alongside the creative arts industry to provide the most realistic assessments to students.
